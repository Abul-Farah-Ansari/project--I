The pizza industry in India has seen remarkable growth in recent years, with an estimated valuation of around INR 3,500 crores. As a result, many national and international F&B brands have recognized the potential for catering to the demand for pizza in both tier 1 and 2 cities. To take advantage of this trend, various pizza franchise groups like Chicago Pizza, Pizza Xpress, US Pizza, Go 69Pizza, Laziz Pizza, Sam’s Pizza, La Pinoz Pizza, and Pizza Station have come up with franchise business opportunities.
